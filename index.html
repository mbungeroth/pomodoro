Hi. This is a pomodoro timer. The pomodoro technique is a useful way to become more productive. You work, focused, for 25 minutes. Focused means focused - not checking your phone or whatever website, even for a few moments (if you do, please hit reset and start again). Once you finish your work session, you may take a short break. After every fourth work session, you get a nice long break. Enjoy!
